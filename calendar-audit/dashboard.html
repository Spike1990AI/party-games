Strategic Recommendations

  1. Protect Deep Work Time: Block 4-6 hours/week for uninterrupted strategic work. August model: 55% deep work is achievable. Current 8-10% is insufficient for executive-level strategic direction.
  2. Consolidate 1:1s: Current 26+ hrs/week in recurring 1:1s is unsustainable. Consider: biweekly cadence for some relationships, group syncs where appropriate, delegate to team leads.
  3. Implement Meeting-Free Days: Designate 1-2 days/week (e.g., Tuesday morning) as no-meeting blocks. August vacation proved Sergio thrives with continuous deep work time.
  4. Batch Meetings by Type: Instead of scattered 1:1s throughout the week, consolidate into "1:1 blocks" (e.g., Monday 14:00-17:00). Reduces context-switching overhead.
  5. Travel Planning Window: Current 47% travel is high but necessary. Cluster travel to specific weeks/months when possible. December model shows benefit of consolidated schedule.
  6. Protect Lunch Blocks: Current 5-8/month insufficient. Aim for 4x/week. Non-negotiable for mental health and energy management in high-stress role.
  7. Audit Late Meetings: 8-18 late meetings/month (after 22:00 GMT) suggests suboptimal global time zone management. Review for unnecessary overlap or delegation opportunities.
  8. Q1 2026 Pilot: Test "Focussed Friday" (no meetings 14:00-17:00 GMT) + "1:1 Mondays" (batch 1:1s) + biweekly lower-priority 1:1s. Track deep work % improvement.
